I'm a UX/UI Designer based in South Wales, UK.

This page showcases my portfolio case studies. This being Menses, a period-tracking app, and Pera, a money-managing app. Please take your time to look through my pieces, and thank you in advance.

My Work

Menses

The goal with Menses was to create a responsive iOS and Android app that could successfully track your period and ovulation cycle. I wanted the app to follow the brief by being able to solve a specific user problem. In this case it would be an app helping to provide women with information about their sexual and reproductive health in conjunction with proving a tracking tool for their cycle needs.

Pera

Pera is a money saving app that helps you track your expenses and budget, for the important purchases you make in life. This app helps to support you in your saving and budgeting goals and subsequently track your daily/weekly expenses.
