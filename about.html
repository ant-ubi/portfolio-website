About Me

Get to know me

Nice to meet you! My name is Antoinette Ubhee and I’m an UX/UI Designer with a background in Neuroscience and Pathology. I am currently based in Swansea, Wales, UK.

I love singing, dancing, and most importantly spending time with my family. I also like to try out new places in the city with my friends and adventure with my dog.

I’m interested in creating visual concepts, that could translate well to various audiences and promote inclusivity. Having an opportunity to make accessible and informative user experiences is something I’m passionate about and would like to pursue moving forward with UX/UI design.
